Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Snake Arcade may process, how that information is used, and what choices players have.

Publisher and Contact

Publisher / app owner: Samuel De La Cruz

Contact: samdlc1991@gmail.com

Gameplay Data

Snake Arcade stores gameplay data on your device so the game can remember settings, scores, owned cosmetics, and related progress.

Ads

Snake Arcade uses Google AdMob to show rewarded ads and interstitial ads. AdMob may process device and ad-request data needed to serve and measure ads. Depending on region and platform behavior, the app may also show privacy or consent choices before requesting ads.

Purchases

Snake Arcade may offer in-app purchases such as Remove Ads. Purchases and restorations are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play billing systems. Snake Arcade does not collect or store your payment card information directly.

Accounts and Optional Sign-In

You can play Snake Arcade without creating an account. If optional sign-in is enabled for leaderboard identity or backup features, the app may process account identifiers and related profile data through Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, Firebase Authentication, and Firebase Firestore.

Analytics

Snake Arcade does not use a separate third-party analytics service in this version unless one is later added and disclosed here.

How Information Is Used

  • Run the game and save progress
  • Restore eligible purchases
  • Show ads and privacy / consent choices
  • Support optional leaderboard identity or backup features
  • Respond to support or legal requests

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob for advertising and consent tooling
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for distribution and in-app purchases
  • Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-In if optional account features are enabled
  • Firebase Authentication and Firestore if optional account features are enabled

Data Retention

  • Local gameplay data remains on your device until you remove the app or clear app data
  • Purchase history is retained by the platform store under its own policies
  • Optional account-backed data may remain in backend systems until it is deleted

Your Choices

  • You can play in guest mode if the app offers it
  • You can choose not to use optional sign-in or backup features
  • You can use any privacy options shown in the app when available
  • You can contact the publisher about support or data-related requests

Children's Privacy

Snake Arcade is intended for a general audience and is not represented as a child-directed app unless the store listing and in-app behavior are explicitly changed to do so.
